About Us



IIT Goa MotorSports is a team developed by the community of the institute's highly motivated students. We aim to step toward sustainable and carbon-neutral energy-powered innovations.

OUR MISSION

The ideology here at IIT Goa MotorSports is to bring new ideas, a different way of thinking, and learning from the successes and failures of not just ourselves but everyone else. This enables us to give a head-to-head competition to all the giant fishes in the sea!
